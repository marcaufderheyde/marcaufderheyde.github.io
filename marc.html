Here's a little information on Group Member Marc:

  1. Marc Auf der Heyde was born in Cape Town, South Africa in 1998. He is 21 years old.
  2. From 2002 to 2011, Marc lived in Blarney, County Cork, Ireland, where he attended primary school.
  3. In 2011, the Auf der Heyde family moved to Berlin, Germany, where Marc completed his High School education.
  4. Marc currently lives in Glasgow, where he attends the University of Glasgow and studies Computing Science.
  5. Marc is the president of the University of Glasgow Harm Reduction Society: Click here to view the Facebook page!
  6. Marc also runs a website for young writers to publish their creative works: Click here to view theyoungauthors!
  7. Marc enjoys writing, computer work, reading (that's still a thing in 2019 I swear), watching movies and spending time with friends.
